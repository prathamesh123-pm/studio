# **App Name**: Pashudhan Insight

## Core Features:

- Dairy/Milking Center Survey Form: Digitize and enable submission of the detailed 'पशुखाद्य सर्वेक्षण फॉर्म' (Cattle Feed Survey Form) to collect comprehensive information from milking centers and dairies, including general info, livestock data, feed usage, brand details, nutritional components, and operational insights.
- Farmer Brand Review Survey: Provide a digital 'कॅटल फीड (पशुखाद्य) ब्रँड रिव्ह्यू व सर्वे प्रश्नावली' for surveyors to collect farmer-specific feedback on cattle feed brands, covering usage, brand choice reasons, quality, pricing, and satisfaction.
- Dynamic Location-Based Feed Insights: An AI-powered tool that analyzes aggregated survey data from selected districts and talukas, including qualitative farmer feedback, to dynamically generate and display summarized insights on the most prevalent cattle feed brands, their ingredient compositions, and overall satisfaction levels in that specific geographic area.
- Secure Survey Data Storage: Securely store all submitted survey data from both forms, including location details (district, taluka, village) and other qualitative and quantitative responses, in a structured database.
- PDF Report Generation: Generate a printable and downloadable PDF report for each completed survey, meticulously including all captured data and surveyor's details such as name and ID.
- Surveyor User Authentication: Enable surveyors to create user accounts, log in securely, and automatically associate their submitted survey data with their unique ID and name for accountability and tracking.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, rustic orange (#C9661F) reflecting the earthiness and richness of agricultural land and produce.
- Background color: A very light, soft cream (#FAF3EC) providing a clean and non-distracting canvas for forms and reports.
- Accent color: A vibrant, warm rosy red (#EC7A89) to highlight important calls to action, form indicators, and positive feedback, evoking vitality.
- All text will use 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif), chosen for its excellent readability and balanced, approachable modern aesthetic suitable for extensive form content and data display.
- Utilize clear, minimalistic line-art icons that are universally understandable, depicting elements like cattle, milk, surveys, and navigation for intuitive user interaction.
- A responsive, clean, and spacious form-centric layout, organizing survey questions into logical sections with clear progress indicators, ensuring ease of data entry on various device sizes.
- Implement subtle, functional animations such as progress spinners for data loading and smooth transitions between form steps to provide feedback and enhance user experience without distraction.
